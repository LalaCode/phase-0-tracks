Magyar Vizsla: Hungarian Royalty

Hungarian Vizslas are a very unique breed of dog, full of quirks which make their humans love them even more.

Originally bred to be both pointer/retriever dogs and loving family companions, the former Hungarian Royal Dog is an energetic and expressive breed.

Vizsla Facts

  • Color: Rusted orange or red with pink nose and clear nails
  • Size: Medium to large
  • Lifespan: 12 - 15 years

Related Breeds

  1. Weimaraner
  2. German Shorthaired Pointer
  3. Rhodesian Ridgeback
